# sisy.blog - Content Hub & SEO Engine

**Domain:** sisy.blog
**Role:** Marketing - Organic content & SEO funnel
**Priority:** Phase 2
**Notion:** See SISY Domain Strategy page

## Purpose
The main content marketing engine for SISY. Publishes articles, interviews, success stories, and industry insights to drive organic search traffic into the SISY ecosystem.

## Key Features / Sections
- Articles on women in speaking & leadership
- Speaker success stories & interviews
- Industry trend pieces
- Event recaps & highlights
- Guest contributor program
- Newsletter signup integration

## Monetization
Organic SEO funnel driving signups to sisy.social and upgrades to sisy.pro.
